## What I built

A **background music manager / DJ** for agentic coding.

- Agent is in control
- Picks, shapes, and transitions the soundtrack around the work


---

## Generative music

- Fun to play with
- Gives the agent real degrees of freedom — tempo, key, density, mood
- The music can actually *respond* to events, not just crossfade over them
- Every run sounds different

---

## Architecture

- SuperCollider - real time audio synthesis engine
- Wrapper web server for control (agent API) and presentation (web dashboard)
- Package as agentic skill / plugin

---

## Musicality

The hard part is making it sound *good*, not just *reactive*.

- Anchored in music theory: scales, chord progressions, voice leading, groove templates that are known to work
- Improvisation within the rules
- Balancing **structure** (so it sounds like music) with **variation** (so it doesn't loop)
